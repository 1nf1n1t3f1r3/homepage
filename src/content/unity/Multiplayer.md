# Multiplayer

So this is the real swamp. Here's where you suddenly find out that there's weird/unexpected reasons for your stuff not to work. You'll find stuff like accidentally controlling multiple characters, sharing an inventory, not being able to do anything, etc. What's arguably even worse is that Physics play out just slightly differently in Multiplayer. It introduces just that slight amount of 'Jank' that permeates everywhere and thus forced me to go back to change Physics that I thought worked perfectly fine before.

Of course 'forced' is a big word. My biggest mistake here is spending 2 months on this rather than just taking the Mirror/Fishnet default NetworkTransform and letting that do it's thing as a 'Good enough' solution. Because, frankly, it is good enough, and certainly better than anything I made.
